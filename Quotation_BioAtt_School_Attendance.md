## 1. Solution Overview

BioAtt is a modern, cost-effective school attendance system that uses face/photo recognition via Android devices. It automates attendance, reporting, and parent notifications, making school management efficient and transparent.

---

## 2. Key Features & Modules

### Multi-Panel Web Portal
- **Admin Panel**
  - Dashboard with statistics (students, teachers, parents, buses, etc.)
  - User management (Admins, Teachers, Parents, Drivers)
  - Teacher, Parent, Student, Class, and Bus management
  - Attendance management and reporting
  - Exam and timetable management
  - Notifications and admission management
  - Secure login/logout and role-based access

- **Teacher Panel**
  - View and manage personal timetable
  - View and manage assigned students
  - Mark and review attendance
  - Enter and view marks/grades
  - Receive and send notifications

- **Parent Panel**
  - View children’s profiles and attendance
  - View marks/grades and timetable
  - Receive notifications from school
  - View and manage fee/payment status

- **Driver Panel**
  - Secure login for bus drivers
  - View assigned routes and students
  - Mark student bus attendance (if implemented)
  - Receive notifications

### Mobile App (Android)
- **Photo/Face Recognition Attendance:** Secure, camera-based attendance (no fingerprint required)
- **Real-time Tracking:** Entry and exit time recording for students and staff
- **Automated Reports:** CSV reports with student/employee details and timestamps
- **WhatsApp & Email Integration:** Automatic report delivery to admins and parents
- **Smart Time Management:** Late arrival and early exit detection
- **Offline Support:** Works without internet; syncs when online
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Modern, intuitive design

### Backend & Integrations
- **Firebase Integration:** Secure authentication, real-time database, and cloud storage
- **Flask Email Backend:** Sends attendance notifications to parents via email
- **Role-Based Access:** Admin, Teacher, Parent, and Driver roles with custom dashboards
- **Secure Authentication:** Email/password login, role-based redirects, and session management

### Other Features
- **Admission Management:** Add new students, assign to classes/sections
- **Class & Timetable Management:** Assign teachers, manage class schedules
- **Bus/Transport Management:** Assign students to buses, manage routes
- **Exam & Marks Management:** Enter, update, and view exam results
- **Notifications:** Send important updates to staff, parents, and drivers
- **Payments:** Track and manage fee payments (parent panel)
- **Customizable:** Easily add new features or modify existing ones
